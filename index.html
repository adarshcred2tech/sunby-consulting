Problems We Solve

Real challenges.
Structured answers.

Inconsistent underwriting standards across the portfolio
We help institutionalise real estate credit assessment frameworks — from risk grading templates to standardised underwriting models — ensuring consistency at every origination desk.
Limited visibility into active portfolio performance
We build structured monitoring and surveillance systems with clear escalation matrices, EWI triggers, and periodic portfolio review formats tailored to your risk appetite.
Stress identified too late — after accounts have already deteriorated
Our Early Warning Indicator frameworks surface risk signals at the SMA-0 stage — before they become SMA-1, SMA-2, or NPA accounts requiring regulatory action.
Weak visibility into stressed account recovery prospects
We provide structured stressed asset monitoring — site inspections, receivable audits, forensic cashflow review, and diversion analysis — to give lenders clear recovery intelligence.
No structured credit policy for real estate lending
We design end-to-end real estate credit policy documents — concentration limits, geography frameworks, product segmentation, developer grading models, and covenant structures — built for regulatory robustness.
Difficulty coordinating with co-lenders in resolution scenarios
We serve as an objective third party in co-lender coordination — providing cost-to-completion analysis, project viability reassessment, and consensus-building support across lender groups.
